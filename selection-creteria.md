# Programme Overview

The BIRE Programme is implemented in two major phases: the Foundational Phase and the Acceleration Phase.

*   **Foundational Phase:** Focuses on recruitment, onboarding, and tailored support for early-stage ventures.
*   **Acceleration Phase:** Focuses on enterprises demonstrating growth traction and readiness to scale, offering investment readiness training and access to financing.

## 2. Foundation Phase Criteria

**Target:** Enterprises in MSEs, with a strong emphasis on women- and youth-led businesses.

### Eligibility Requirements (Must meet all):

*   Registered, located, and operating in Kenya.
*   Recognized as a legal entity.
*   Operational for at least 2 years.
*   Has management books of accounts for at least 1 year.
*   Revenues between KES 500,000 â€“ KES 3,000,000 per annum.
*   Currently employs at least 3 employees.

### Scoring Matrix (Total: 100 Marks):

#### Commercial Viability (20 Marks)
*   **Proof of Sales (Last 1 year revenue):** >2M (10), 1M-2M (5), 500k-1M (2).
*   **Number of Customers:** >401 (10), 200-400 (5), 1-200 (2).
*   **External Fundraising (Received):** Yes (10), No (5).

#### Business Model (10 Marks)
*   **Description:** New (10), Relatively New (5), Existing (2).

#### Market Potential (30 Marks)
*   **Relative Pricing:** Lower (7), Equal (4), Higher (1).
*   **Product Differentiation:** New (8), Relatively new (5), Existing (2).
*   **Threat of Substitutes:** Low (7), Moderate (4), High (0).
*   **Ease of Market Entry:** Low (8), Moderate (5), High (1).

#### Social Impact (40 Marks)
*   **Environmental Impact:** Clearly Defined (15), Neutral (10), Not Defined (5).
*   **Special Groups Employed (Women, Youth, PWD):** >10 (15), 6-9 (10), 5 (5).
*   **Business Compliance:** Fully Compliant (10), Partially Compliant (3), Not clear (1).

**Pass Mark:** An enterprise needs a minimum of 70 Marks to be eligible.

## 3. Acceleration Phase Criteria

**Target:** Ventures demonstrating growth traction and readiness to scale.

### Eligibility Requirements (Must meet all):

*   Registered, located, and operating in Kenya.
*   Recognized as a legal entity.
*   Operational for at least 2 years.
*   Has at least 1 year of certified audited books of accounts.
*   Revenues above KES 3,000,000 per annum.
*   Job opportunities created for 5-20 employees.

### Scoring Matrix (Total: 100 Marks):

#### Revenues & Growth (20 Marks)
*   **Revenue:** >5M (5), 3.5M-5M (3), 3M-3.5M (1).
*   **Years of Operation:** >4 years (5), >3 years (3), 2 years (1).
*   **Future Potential Sales Growth:** High (5), Moderate (3), Low (1).
*   **Funds Raised:** Yes (5), No (1).

#### Impact Potential (20 Marks)
*   **Current Youth/Women/PWD employed:** >10 (10), 6-9 (6), 5 (3).
*   **Potential to create new jobs (Women/PWD/Youth):** High (10), Moderate (6), Low (3).

#### Scalability (20 Marks)
*   **Market Differentiation:** Truly Unique (5), Provably Better (3), Undifferentiated (1).
*   **Competitive Advantage:** High (5), Moderate (3), Low (1).
*   **Offering Focus:** Outcome Focused (5), Solution Focused (3), Feature Focused (1).
*   **Sales & Marketing Integration:** Fully Integrated (5), Aligned (3), No Alignment (1).

#### Social & Environmental Impact (20 Marks)
*   **Social Impact (Household income):** High (6), Moderate (4), None (0).
*   **Supplier Involvement:** Direct (6), Network (3), None (1).
*   **Environmental Impact:** High (6), Moderate (4), Low (0).

#### Business Model (20 Marks)
*   **Uniqueness:** High (7), Moderate (3), Low (1).
*   **Customer Value Proposition:** High (7), Moderate (3), Low (1).
*   **Competitive Advantage Strength:** High (6), Moderate (3), Low (1).

**Pass Mark:** An enterprise needs a minimum of 70 Marks to be eligible

## 1. Strategy & Psychological Flow
The goal of this landing page is to convert visitors (Micro and Small Enterprise owners) into applicants. The flow of information is designed to answer their internal questions in a specific order:

1.  **Credibility:** "Is this real? Who is behind it?" (Hero Section & Partners)
2.  **Value Proposition:** "What do I get out of this?" (Benefits & Offerings)
3.  **Self-Selection:** "Is this meant for me?" (Tracks & Eligibility)
4.  **Trust:** "Has this worked before?" (Impact & Stats)
5.  **Action:** "How do I start?" (Timeline & Application CTA)

## 3. Technical & Design Notes

*   **Mobile Responsiveness:** Most MSE owners will access this via mobile phones. The "Apply" buttons must be sticky or easily accessible on mobile screens.
*   **Speed:** Images must be optimized (WebP format) to load fast on 4G/3G networks common in rural Kenya.
*   **Accessibility:** Use high-contrast colors (Hand in Hand Blue & Yellow/White) for readability. Ensure all images have Alt Text.
*   **Language:** Keep English simple and professional. Consider a toggle for "Kiswahili" version if possible for inclusivity.

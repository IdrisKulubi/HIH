# BIRE Application Forms & Questions

## Part 1: General Eligibility & Routing (All Applicants)

### Section 1: Business Profile

**Applicant Details:**
- Name
- ID/Passport
- Gender
- Phone
- Email

**Business Details:**
- County
- Sector/Value chain
- Brief Description
- Customer Problem Solved

**Registration:**
- **Is business registered in Kenya?** (No = Auto-disqualify)
- **Legal Type:**
    - Limited Co
    - Partnership
    - Cooperative
    - Self-Help/CBO
    - Sole Proprietorship
    - *(Not registered = Auto-disqualify)*
- **Upload:** Registration Certificate (Mandatory)

### Section 2: Years of Operation

**How long has the business been operational?**
- < 1 year (Auto-disqualify)
- 1–2 years (Foundation only)
- > 2 years (Eligible for both)

### Section 3: Books of Accounts

- **Do you have 1 year of books/bank statements/Mpesa statements?** (No = Auto-disqualify)
- **Do you have 1 year of audited financial statements?** (Yes = Eligible for Accelerator; No = Foundation only)

### Section 4: Revenue Levels

**Total annual sales last year:**
- < KES 500,000 (Auto-disqualify)
- KES 500,000 – 3,000,000 (Foundation Track)
- > KES 3,000,000 (Acceleration Track)

### Section 5: Employment

**Full-time employees:**
- 1–4 (Foundation Track)
- 5–20 (Acceleration Track)
- > 20 (Acceleration Track)

## Part 2: Foundation Track Application Form

### Section B: Business Model (10 Marks)

- **Describe your business model** (How does the business make money?)
- **Options:**
    - Innovative concept
    - Relatively Innovative
    - Existing/well-established

### Section C: Commercial Viability (20 Marks)

- **Revenue:** Total revenue for last financial year? (>2M, 1-2M, 500k-1M)
- **Customers:** Customers served in past 12 months? (>401, 200-400, 1-200)
- **Text:** Describe key customer segments.
- **Funding:** Received external funding (loans/grants)? (Yes/No). List funders if yes.

### Section D: Market Potential (30 Marks)

- **Pricing:** Compare pricing to competitors (Lower, Equal, Higher). Explain.
- **Differentiation:** How unique is the product? (New, Relatively new, Similar). Describe uniqueness.
- **Substitutes:** Intensity of competition? (Low, Moderate, High). Overview of competitors.
- **Entry:** Ease for others to enter market? (Low, Moderate, High).

### Section E: Social Impact (40 Marks)

- **Environment:** Does business conserve environment? (Clearly defined, Minimal, Not defined). Provide examples.
- **Inclusion:** Number of women, youth, PWD employees? (>10, 6-9, 5). Provide breakdown.
- **Compliance:** Status? (Fully compliant, Partially compliant). Upload optional documents.

### Section F: Document Uploads

- Registration certificate
- One year financial records/bank statements/Mpesa statements
- Evidence of sales
- Photos of products/operations

### Section G: Declaration

- Certify accuracy.
- Name, Signature, Date.

## Part 3: Acceleration Track Application Form

### Section B: Revenue & Growth (20 Marks)

- **Revenue:** Total annual revenue last FY? (>5M, 3.5-5M, 3-3.5M)
- **Upload:** Audited financial statements (Mandatory).
- **History:** Years operational? (>4, 3-4, 2). Provide history of growth.
- **Projections:** Projected sales growth next 6-12 months? (High, Moderate, Low). Explain basis.
- **Funding:** External funding received? (Yes/No). List details.

### Section C: Impact Potential (20 Marks)

- **Current Jobs:** Number of women, youth, PWD employed? (>10, 6-9, 5). Provide breakdown.
- **Future Jobs:** Potential to create new jobs in 12-24 months? (High >5, Moderate 2-5, Low 1-2).

### Section D: Scalability (20 Marks)

- **Differentiation:** How differentiated is product? (Truly unique, Provably better, Undifferentiated). Explain strengths.
- **Advantage:** Level of competitive advantage? (High, Moderate, Low). Describe sources.
- **Focus:** How offering addresses outcomes? (Outcome-focused, Solution-focused, Feature-focused).
- **Integration:** Sales and marketing alignment? (Fully integrated, Aligned, Not aligned). Describe approach.

### Section E: Social & Environmental Impact (20 Marks)

- **Social:** Contribution to improvements (jobs, income)? (High, Moderate, None).
- **Suppliers:** How do you engage suppliers? (Direct, Network-based, No clear engagement). Describe support.
- **Environment:** Environmental conservation practices? (Clearly defined, Minimal, Not defined). Provide examples.

### Section F: Business Model (20 Marks)

- **Uniqueness:** How unique is the model? (High, Moderate, Low). Describe differences.
- **Value Prop:** Strength of value proposition? (High, Moderate, Low). Why customers buy.
- **Advantage Strength:** Rate strength/barriers protecting position (High, Moderate, Low).

### Section G: Document Uploads

- Registration certificate
- Audited financial statements (Mandatory)
- Tax compliance (Optional)
- Photos or brochure

### Section H: Declaration

- Certify accuracy.
- Name, Signature, Date.
